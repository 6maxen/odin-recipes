Rice

Recipe for how to make rice

This recipe is on how to make the perfect rice from beginning to end!

Ingredients

  • Rice Cooker
  • Uncooked Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour 1(* however many people) cup of rice into your rice cooker
  2. Head to the sink and fill your rice with water while mixing your rice to clean it
  3. Drain the dirty water
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 at least three times to thoroughly clean your rice
  5. Fill your rice cooker with 2(* however many people) cups of water
  6. Select the cook option on your rice cooker
  7. Wait till your rice cooker finishes and enjoy!
