Salmon

Recipe for how to make salmon

This recipe is on how to make the perfect salmon from beginning to end!

Ingredients

  • Oven/Toaster/Pan
  • Pan
  • Butter
  • Salmon filets
  • Onion/Garlic/Pepper
  • (Not Required) Basil Leaves
  • (Not Required) Lemon

Steps

  1. (If you're using a pan) Put butter onto your pan
  2. Add butter on top of your salmon filet
  3. Cook for about 10min on medium heat
  4. Add onion/garlic/pepper/basil leaves on top of your salmon filet
  5. Cook for another 10min on medium heat
  6. Place salmon on a plate
  7. Squeeze lemon on top of your salmon filet
