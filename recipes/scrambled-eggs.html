Scrambled Eggs

Recipe for how to make scrambled eggs

This recipe is on how to make the perfect scrambled eggs from beginning to end!

Ingredients

  • Bowl
  • Pan
  • Spoon
  • Spatula or Ladle
  • Egg(s)

Steps

  1. Crack open your egg(s) and put them in a bowl
  2. Mix the egg with your spoon
  3. Once thoroughly mixed, pour the egg onto a pan
  4. Heat the pan on medium-low heat while mixing with a Spatula/Ladle
  5. Turn off the stove and pour eggs onto your bowl and enjoy!
